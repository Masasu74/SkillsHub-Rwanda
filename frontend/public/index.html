An end-to-end learning platform for students and mentors

SkillsHub brings together course creation, enrollment management, and progress tracking so learning journeys feel personal, measurable, and inspiring.

Curated course catalog

Discover modules by category, level, and keyword—optimized for low bandwidth and mobile-first access.

One-click enrollment & dashboards

Students join courses instantly while instructors and admins monitor cohorts, milestones, and completion rates.

Progress tracking & module feedback

Track progress down to module level, capture reflections, and celebrate learners as they hit 100% completion.
